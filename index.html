The studio

Poplar Music Studios is an independent, owner-run room in East London built for people who actually make records — not a corporate rehearsal chain.

No day-rate minimums, no clock-watching, and a live room set up so a session can go from writing to tracking without resetting the whole rig.

Co-owners run the desk in rotation, so whoever's confirming your booking has almost certainly used the room themselves that week.
